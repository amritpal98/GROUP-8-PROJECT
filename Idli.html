Recipe of Idli

Ingredients:
1/2 cup urad dal 1 cup rice 2 tbsp thick poha or flattened rice Water, as required Salt, to taste
Method:Wash the rice and dal several times, and then soak them in water for 3-6 hours. Soak the poha for 2 hours. 2. Drain the water from the rice, dal and poha. Transfer them to a large mixie jar with some salt, and grind while adding water gradually. You should get a smooth and frothy batter that is just slightly thick. 3. Let the batter rest for 6-12 hours in a warm place. This will allow it to ferment. 4. Boil water in the idli steamer, and grease the idli moulds or plates with some vegetable oil. Mix the batter well, and pour on the idli plates. Place the idli plates in the steamer and cook for 10 minutes at a medium-high flame. Remove from the steamer and serve. This recipe is super-easy, and once you do know how to master it, you can try your hand at some more delicious idli recipes. Yes, there are many idli variations you can try.
